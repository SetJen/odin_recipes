Hamburgers

Description

This is a quick and easy way to make a hamburger.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Cheese
  • Buns
  • ketchup

Steps

  1. Form ground beef into patties
  2. Fry patties on stove until brown inside and out
  3. Add cheese on top of patties and let melt
  4. Serve on bun with ketchup and enjoy
