lasagna

description

This is a quick and easy recipe to make a delicious lasagna.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Mozarella Cheese
  • Egg
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Cook noodles and beef
  2. Mix ricotta, egg, salt, and pepper
  3. Layer sauce, noodles, ricotta mix, mozzarella. Repeat.
  4. Bake and 375 degrees F
